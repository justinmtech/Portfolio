About Me

I am a passionate and hard-working individual who loves solving problems. I ran a business in the gaming space for 7 years, which eventually lead me to get interested in programming. I started out by learning Java, and two years later, I am now confident enough in my skills to begin my job search.

I prefer working with back-end software, but I am able to create web front-ends as well. Ultimately I would like to be a full-stack developer, and that is what I am working towards.

Currently I work in residential construction and remodelling. I like to think of coding as digital building, because there are so many similarities between the two vocations while appearing completely different in practice.

In my free time I like to read philosophy and learn about science, technology and human evolution. Life is a beautiful thing and my primary goal as an individual is to learn as much as I can about the world around me, my place in it, and what I can do to make it better for myself and others.
