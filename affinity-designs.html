Affinity Designs

General Summary

Affinity Designs (2013-2020) was a small company I founded and managed that created unique social gaming experiences. At its core, it was a multiplayer game server that attracted 250,000 unique players over the 7 year period we were in business. It was a relatively popular free-to-play game where players could buy items in the game through our website with real money.

Our main product was a Minecraft server that players could join, play on and spend money. Minecraft allows their multiplayer server software to be modified, so an ecosystem of "modded minecraft servers" was born to cater to the growing demand for multiplayer Minecraft server experiences.

There is a huge community of players that play Minecraft servers these days, and we were one of the early movers in that space.

How was it made?

We created a "modded" Minecraft server using both public and private software. Minecraft servers are modified through the use of plugins. The original Minecraft was made in Java, and plugins are made in Java as well.

Designing and deploying a professional Minecraft server is similar to designing a game or a web application. It requires proper planning and execution to be a good experience for players.

Our product started out as just one server, and it was a Factions server. Our Factions server was a competitive game with an economy, teams, base building, raiding, boss fights and more. It was our most popular server which peaked at around 150 concurrent players on one instance in 2017. We decided to expand due to demands from our growing playerbase, so we added another server called Survival. Survival was a modified type of Minecraft Survival that included an economy, party system, custom XP and leveling system and various other additions to make Minecraft Survival more fun and multiplayer friendly.
